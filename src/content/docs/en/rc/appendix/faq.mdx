## What is YAMP?
YAMP is Yet Another M* Platform.
Allowing the creation of gamemodes like FiveM, RageMP or alt:V

## Is YAMP Open Source?
Core parts will remain closed source, but the website, runtimes, and docs will be open source.

## Is YAMP free
The platform will always be free, no features behind a paywall.

# Will this only work on the Enhanced Edition?
We aim to support Enhanced Edition, but we are now focusing the legacy version.
